Before you hire anyone

5 questions
most people skip.

Ask any bricklayer these before you sign. Vague answers are where shortcuts hide.

01

What mortar mix for this job?

The wrong mix cracks or stains within a year. A pro specs the mortar for your bricks and the exposure.

02

How are the footings set out?

Brickwork is only as good as what it sits on. Proper footings and set-out stop cracking down the track.

03

What is included in the quote?

Materials, set-out, cleanup and finishing. A cheap quote almost always left one of these out.

04

Who is on the tools, licensed?

You should know who turns up on site, and that they hold the licence for the work.

05

What if a course is not true?

If a line is not right, it comes down and gets laid again. That is the standard.
