Before you hire anyone

5 questions
most people skip.

Ask any painter these before you sign. Vague answers are where shortcuts hide.

01

What primer for this surface?

The wrong primer means paint that peels. A pro names the exact product for your surface.

02

How many coats, and drying time?

Two coats minimum, full drying time between. One thick coat shows within a year.

03

What prep is in the price?

Wash, sand, fill, caulk, mask. A cheap quote almost always skipped this.

04

Who is on the tools, insured?

You should know who turns up at your home, and that they are insured.

05

What if it is not right?

A real guarantee is in writing. Ours is seven years. Not right? We come back.
