Before you hire anyone

5 questions
most people skip.

Ask any painter these before you sign. A professional answers without hesitation. Everyone else gets vague, and vague is where the shortcuts hide.

01

What primer will you use on this surface?

Primer is not optional, it is the foundation. The wrong primer on timber, plaster or a repainted surface leads to adhesion failure. A professional names the product for your surface.

02

How many coats, and what is the drying time between them?

One thick coat is a shortcut that shows within a year. We do two coats minimum, and we wait the full recoat time. Rushing this is the single most common cause of a job that fails early.

03

What preparation is included in the price?

Washing, sanding, filling, caulking, masking. Prep is most of the work and most of the result. If a quote is suspiciously cheap, this is almost always what was left out.

04

Who is actually on the tools, and are they insured?

You should know who turns up at your home and that they carry insurance. We tell you exactly who is on your job, start to finish. No mystery subcontractors.

05

What happens if something is not right?

A real guarantee is specific and in writing. Ours is seven years on workmanship. If a line is not right, we come back and make it right. That is the whole point of hiring properly.
