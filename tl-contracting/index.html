Before you hire anyone

5 questions
most people skip.

Ask any earthworks or landscaping contractor these before you sign. Vague answers are where cost blowouts hide.

01

Where does the water go?

Every shaped surface sends water somewhere. A pro can point to where, and what happens in a big Waikato downpour.

02

What happens to the spoil?

Cart-away is often the biggest hidden cost. Is spoil reused on site, or trucked, and is that in the price?

03

Are services located first?

Power, fibre and water lines run where you least expect. Locating them before digging is basic, and skipped surprisingly often.

04

What is in the quote?

Machine time, cartage, metal, topsoil, compaction, cleanup. A cheap quote usually left one of these out.

05

Who reinstates the site?

Tracks, lawn damage and gateways. Ask who makes it good afterwards, and get it in writing.
